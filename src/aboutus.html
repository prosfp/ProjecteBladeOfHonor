¿Who we are?

Blade of Honor, where our passion about the rich heritage of ancient Japan come to life

We are two young enthusiasts with a deep passion for katanas and ancient Japan. Inspired by the samurai and Japanese traditions, we’ve dedicated ourselves to sharing this rich cultural heritage with others

Our mission is to offer the finest katanas and create a space where the art, history, and honor of the samurai can be experienced firsthand.
